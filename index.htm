RF Engineering

RF engineers work with devices that transmit or receive radio waves, including wireless equipment, radios, and cellular phones.

Civil Engineering

Civil engineers design, construct, and maintain of the physical and naturally built environments, including public works such as roads, bridges, airports, sewerage systems, pipelines, and railways.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering is a technical discipline concerned with the study, design and application of equipment, devices and systems which use electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.
Embedded Systems Engineering

Embedded Software Engineers design various devices and machines that integrating software with non-computer devices.
Biomedical Engineering

Biomedical engineering improves human health through cross-disciplinary activities that integrate the engineering sciences with the biomedical sciences and clinical practice.
